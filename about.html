Built Different. On Purpose.

IsoWatt makes portable LiFePO4 power systems engineered for real-world use. No compromises. No mass production. Every unit assembled by hand in Bellefonte, PA.

Our Story

IsoWatt started as a personal need: reliable, portable power for work in remote locations. Existing options were either underpowered, overpriced, or came with compromises that didn't fit the job. So we built our own.

What began as a one-off project evolved into a business. We realized other people needed what we had built — systems that just work, built with quality components, and backed by someone who actually understands the product.

Why LiFePO4?

LiFePO4 chemistry is what industrial power systems use. It's stable, safe, and lasts. 2,000+ charge cycles. No thermal runaway. No overnight failures. It costs more upfront, but it's the chemistry we trust with our own equipment.

Built to Order

We don't keep inventory. We don't mass-produce. Every IsoStation and IsoPoint is assembled, wired, and load-tested by hand before it ships. That means longer lead times, but it also means quality control that's personal.

Local Support

You're not talking to a call center. Issues get escalated to someone who built the unit. Questions get answered by someone who uses them in the field.
